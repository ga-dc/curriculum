Who I am

Matt Curtis isn't just the header of this website; it's also my name! I am a native Northern Virginian, living in Centreville, Vienna, and currently Fairfax. Graduated Westfield High School in Chantilly, VA and then James Madison University with a bachelor's degree in Kinesology, concentration of sport and recreation management. I had been working business to consumer sales in the fitness industry since college, then I left to work business to business advertising sales for a technology company. So now, what am I doing here?

How did I get here

I have always been curious about computer programming since a young age but never acted on that emotion. While working advertising sales for a technology company, I found that I was more interested in the technology component instead of the sales aspect of the business. So, for the past couple months I have been getting my feet wet with coding to see if it is something I would enjoy. Now, I have the opportunity to invest full-time into my education to change my line of work.

Where I'm going

I don't consider web development as a career change, more as a change in type of work. In the short-term, I want to finish the course and immediately begin working for a company to better refine my skill set. The ability to continuously learn new skills is what is the most appealing to me about web development and I plan to eventually incorporate all my sales, marketing, and web development experience into the sports world.
